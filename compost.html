Compost Drop-Off in Philadelphia

Drop off your food scraps and compostables at the following locations:

  • 🟢 South Philly Green Drop – 9am–12pm, Sundays
  • 🟢 Fairmount Neighborhood Compost – 10am–1pm, Saturdays
  • 🟢 University City Drop-Off – 8am–11am, Fridays

Accepted: fruits, vegetables, coffee grounds, egg shells.
Not accepted: meat, dairy, plastic.
